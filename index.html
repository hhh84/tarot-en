Frequently Asked Questions

Is tarot accurate?▼
Tarot is a psychological tool that helps you view your situation from different angles. The result depends on your own intuition and judgment — it is not an absolute prophecy. Think of it as a mirror reflecting your inner thoughts and feelings.
How many times can I read per day?▼
It is generally advised not to repeat a reading for the same question. If you feel calm and want to try again later, that is fine. There is no strict daily limit, but approach each reading with a peaceful mindset and avoid becoming dependent on it.
What does a reversed card mean?▼
A reversed card represents blocked energy, an internal state, or an invitation to understand the card from the opposite perspective. It does not necessarily carry a negative meaning — it often reminds you to pay attention to a side you have been ignoring. It may also indicate an energy that is dormant or in need of integration.
Which deck should I use?▼
This app uses the classic Rider-Waite Tarot, the most widely known and studied deck. Once you become familiar with it, you may explore other decks that suit your personal style. Different decks have different artistic styles, but their core meanings remain consistent.
What do I need to prepare for a reading?▼
The most important thing is a calm mind. Find a quiet space, hold your question clearly in your thoughts, and trust your intuition. No special tools or rituals are required — tarot is simply a tool to help you connect with your inner wisdom.
